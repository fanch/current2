NuTyX licenses

NuTyX is build around various licenses

The packages of NuTyX

As NuTyX is a GNU/linux distribution, it contains software written by a large number of developers around the world. Therefore, you will discover, all packages will have different licenses chosen by their authors. To know the type of the license used, please consult the sources code of the concerned package.

The Scripts

The scripts coming from the project Linux From Scratch are under the http://lfs.traduc.org/view/lfs...tml>Creative Commons License 2.0. license. Some scripts have been adjusted for the needs of NuTyX by The Chief Administrator and Founder Thierry Nuttens.

The Installer (MINI iso)

The installer is build from scratch by The Chief Administrator and Founder Thierry Nuttens and is under license http://www.gnu.org/copyleft/gpl.html
